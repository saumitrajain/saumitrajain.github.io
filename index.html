About Me



Hi I am Saumitra Jain, my name in my native language means 100 friends which is exactly my nature. I love to connect with people from different backgrounds to get an insight of their work. I am curious and creative by nature. I want to develop things that are unique and to develop my skills in a continuum.

I am currently working with Infosys as a developer. Last year I graduated from State University Of New York-University at Albany.

In Summer 2014, I was employed with NYC DEP as a software engineering intern. In this role I have to automate the internal process by creating application which will enable the employees for effortless work. Our most exciting work to date is the Web application LIMS (Laboratory Information Managements System) which will allow the real time updation of Laboratory Data across NYC-DEP on the SQL Server. Not only did we have an enormous learning while creating the application, but also we found it rewarding to see the results.

I’m truly passionate about my work and always eager to learn more to grow my technical knowledge. While I enjoy all aspects of my job, I think my favorite stage of a project is interaction with clients to know their objectives from the project. I always tend to follow a creative approach towards work and always express my ideas during this process. As we go through that collaborative process, the ideas start to flow and that’s always the fun part.

I’m always interested in hearing from former colleagues, managers, or just interesting creative folk, so feel free to contact me.
